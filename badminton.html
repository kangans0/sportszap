ABOUT

Badminton is a popular racquet sport played on a rectangular court divided by a net. The game can be played in singles or doubles,
with players using lightweight racquets to hit a shuttlecock back and forth over the net. The objective is to score points by landing
the shuttlecock in the opponent's court. Matches are typically best of three games, with each game played to 21 points, requiring a
two-point margin for victory. Badminton is an Olympic sport and is enjoyed recreationally as well as competitively in professional
leagues and international tournaments. The sport provides numerous health benefits, including improved cardiovascular fitness, agility,
and reflexes. With its fast-paced nature and widespread appeal, badminton continues to be a popular and engaging sport globally.
