ABOUT

Hockey is a fast-paced team sport played on a rectangular field or rink. Two teams, each comprising six players,including a
goalkeeper, use a small, hard ball and curved sticks to score goals by shooting the ball into the opposing team's net. The game is divided into periods,
and the team with the most goals at the end wins. Hockey is played on various surfaces, including grass, turf, and ice, with variations like field hockey
and ice hockey.It is popular globally, with major tournaments such as the Olympic Games and the Hockey World Cup showcasing the skill and athleticism of
players.Hockey has a rich history and is widely enjoyed in countries around the world. It is known for its fast-paced and exciting nature, requiring
players to be agile, skilled, and strategic in their approach.The sport has a significant presence in major international competitions, including
the Olympic Games and various world championships.
