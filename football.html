ABOUT

Football, also known as soccer in some regions, is a globally popular team sport played between two teams of 11 players each. The objective is to score
goals by getting the ball into the opposing team's net.Matches consist of two halves, each lasting 45 minutes, with a halftime break. Players, assigned
specific positions, work together to move the ball across the field and score. The sport is governed by rules, including fouls and offside, and major
international tournaments like the FIFA World Cup captivate millions of fans worldwide. Football's universal appeal transcends cultural boundaries, making it
one of the most watched and played sports globally.
